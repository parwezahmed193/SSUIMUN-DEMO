Sri Sri Ravishankar

Gurudev founded The Art of Living as an international, non-profit, educational and humanitarian organization.

MISSION

His mission is to offer powerful tools for self-development and education to eliminate stress and foster a sence of well- being.

Gurudev envisions a violence free and stress-free society. His vision has expressed itself in numerous service initiatives that have inspired volunteers worldwide.
